Welcome to Medical Terminology, uncoding the jargon.

In ths short lesson you will learn how to better understand the language of doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals.

What ARE they on about?

If you've ever been confused by your health provider, it might be that you are just not speaking their language. Medical professionals use specific words to understand and communicate your body and the way it works or the way it is not working.

Because the words are very specific and understood by other healthcare professionals, they are like a code that helps each provider understand exactly what is happening and what should happen. Unfortunately, most of us don't know the code, are are unable to understand what we're being told.
