Bob's Image Gallery

Each of these images was produced with Daz Studio and post work in ClipStudio Paint and/or Photoshop.
The images were initially produced for submitted to the DC Comics Community for Fan Art. While the club is kind of an unoffical community, it's a great place to have fun with other geeks.
While I realize that these images may not present the "professional" image many corporations would be expecting, I do think that they demonstrate my ability to learn and employ new artist techniques for the purpose of story telling.
